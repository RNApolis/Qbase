Cite us

Any published work which has made use of the database should cite the following paper:

T. Zok, J. Miskiewicz, P. Pielacinska, N. Kraszewska, M. Szachniuk. ONQUADRO: a database of tetrads and quadruplexes, submitted for publication


The following papers can be also cited:

  • M. Popenda, J. Miskiewicz, J. Sarzynska, T. Zok, M. Szachniuk (2020) Topology-based classification of tetrads and quadruplex structures, Bioinformatics 36(4):1129–1134.

  • T. Zok, M. Popenda, M. Szachniuk (2020) ElTetrado: a tool for identification and classification of tetrads and quadruplexes, BMC Bioinformatics 21:40.
