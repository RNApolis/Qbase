Citations

Any published work which has made use of the database should cite the following paper:

T. Zok, P. Pielacinska, J. Miskiewicz, M. Popenda, M. Szachniuk. QBASE: a database of tetrads and quadruplexes with ONZ taxonomy, submitted for publication

The following papers can be also cited:

M. Popenda, J. Miskiewicz, J. Sarzynska, T. Zok, M. Szachniuk. Novel classification of tetrads and quadruplex structure, submitted for publication.

T. Zok, M. Popenda, J. Miskiewicz, M. Szachniuk. ElTetrado: a tool for identification and ONZ classification of quadruplexes, submitted for publication.
