About

Quadruplexes are characteristic tertiary structure motifs that form in nucleic acids. They participate in various biological processes and they are promising therapeutic targets in many strategies of drug development

Here, we present a database of tetrads and quadruplexes found in PDB-deposited structures. We show their assignment to ONZ categories, unique dot-bracket encoding, and arc diagrams. Both dot-bracket and graphical representations are adjusted to ONZ taxonomy which is based on the secondary structure topology and allows to classify canonical and non-canonical quadruplexes found in unimolecular structures.
